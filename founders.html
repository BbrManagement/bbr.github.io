BBR Founders

Here you can find information about the founders of Bible Beholders Reborn (Alex, Brack and Will)!

Our Founders are Will and Alex McCartha, and Brack Jones. Youth Members from the University Church Of Christ. They founded BBR as a place where young christians could come together and interact, bond and learn with one another. 

Originally they founded BBR as a small goup within their area and friends they knew, however as of October 1st 2019, they've launched BBR as a Nationwide Online Youth Group for the Church Of Christ community. (The following descriptions/bio's are self-written and in no way reflect the opinions or views of the BBR Orginization)

Alex McCartha

I started BBR with Brack and Will because I’ve always had an intrest in the Bible and things of a spiritual nature, when we made BBR we only meant for it to be a small thing between friends, but then we saw that we had the opertunity to make BBR a place for Christian youth to come to enjoy fellowship and have a place to talk. I try to be the theripist of the group, I am always willing and egar to talk when people want/need to, no matter what people are struggling with I always try my hardest to help in every way possible. In my life I plan to go to Faulkner University and eventually become a minister and spread the word of God.

Will McCartha

I'm a 15 year old Christian from Montgomery AL. I founded BBR with my brother and Brack as a fun little group within our friend area, so that we could socialize and hang out. However, with the members in the group, I've come to learn a lot about myself, Christ, and my own faith. I've worked to continue pushing this group to be better, so that we might be able to share the amazing experiences and abilities I've grown to know. I'm a Student Developer, and your normal techie.

Brack Jones
