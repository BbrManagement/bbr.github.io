Our Goals

We are a group of young Christians coming together to help build one another up in Christ, and to enjoy fellowship with one another. We host Bible Readings, Singing Events, and Devotionals. Here we list our schedule along with resources for both new and lifelong Christians. 

The goals of BBR is to help young Christians with finding their own faith, and getting through lifes struggles. We aim to be a place where those in need can find help, and can find fellowship in Christ. Our goal is to try and build others up in Christ, so that they might grow to be strong in their faith.

As stated in our other page, we host regular events such as Devotionals, Singing Nights and Bible Readings. These events are designed for fellowship and faith building. We host these so we can gather together, interact and learn more about the inspired word of the Lord.
