About our Group

Here you can find information about who we are and what we do!

Bible Bro's Reborn (Hereby referred to as BBR), is a group of young Christians who have come together to aid eachother in growing with Christ, learn and discover more about Christ and the Bible, and to help our fellow Christians in their worldy struggles.

Our group's icon/mascot is the Pheonix. A bird from Greek Mythology, commonly associated with the Sun. We've chosen this creature due to the fact a phoenix obtains new life by arising from the ashes of its predecessor. In the same sense, as Christians we're baptised into the Holy Spirit. In doing so, the man of earthly ways passes away, and from him, and from Christ, we're born again.

What We Do

Our group hosts events regularly, things such as Devotionals, Bible Readings and Song Events. We also just offer a communal place where you can hang out, form close friendships and bonds, and just generally have fun with your family in Christ.
